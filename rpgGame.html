Here's how you play the game


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four dogs above.
    • By clicking on a dog, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
    • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
    • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
    • The value of each dog is hidden from you until you click on it.
    • Refresh the page to start over since I don't have a reset button for you.
